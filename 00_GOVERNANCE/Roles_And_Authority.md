# Purpose

This document defines the organizational roles, responsibilities, authority levels, delegation rules, reporting relationships, and accountability framework for VAYRA OS Enterprise.

It ensures that every human contributor and AI agent operates within clearly defined responsibilities and decision-making boundaries.

---

# Mission

To establish a transparent organizational authority model that enables effective leadership, responsible decision-making, efficient collaboration, and scalable enterprise operations.

---

# Objectives

The Roles and Authority framework aims to:

- Define organizational roles.
- Clarify responsibilities.
- Establish authority limits.
- Standardize delegation.
- Improve accountability.
- Reduce role conflicts.
- Support governance compliance.
- Enable scalable growth.

---

# Scope

This framework applies to:

- Executive Leadership
- AI Agents
- Department Heads
- Project Teams
- Business Operations
- Design Operations
- Technical Operations
- Finance
- HR
- Marketing
- Future Organizational Roles

---

# Authority Principles

## Accountability

Every responsibility must have a clearly assigned owner.

---

## Delegation

Authority may be delegated, but accountability remains with the original owner unless formally reassigned.

---

## Separation of Duties

Critical business activities should involve appropriate review and approval to reduce operational risk.

---

## Escalation

When authority limits are exceeded, decisions must be escalated to the appropriate authority level.

---

## Collaboration

Cross-functional work should involve relevant departments while respecting defined authority boundaries.

---

# Executive Roles

## Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Responsibilities

- Enterprise vision and strategy
- Business governance
- Executive decision-making
- Organizational growth
- Final approval authority

Authority

- Approve strategic initiatives
- Approve governance changes
- Approve enterprise architecture
- Approve executive AI agents
- Final authority for enterprise decisions

Reports To

- Business Owner

---

## Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Responsibilities

- Daily operations
- Project delivery
- Resource management
- Process optimization
- Operational performance

Authority

- Approve operational workflows
- Allocate operational resources
- Resolve operational issues
- Monitor delivery performance

Reports To

- CEO

---

## Chief Design Officer (CDO)

Responsibilities

- Design standards
- Technical standards
- AI implementation
- Repository architecture
- Design innovation

Authority

- Approve design standards
- Manage AI implementation
- Maintain enterprise architecture
- Govern technical documentation

Reports To

- CEO

---

# Department Heads

Each Department Head is responsible for:

- Department planning
- Resource management
- Team performance
- SOP implementation
- Quality assurance
- Compliance

Authority

- Approve departmental workflows
- Manage departmental resources
- Review deliverables
- Escalate strategic matters

---

# AI Agent Roles

Every AI agent must have documented:

- Purpose
- Responsibilities
- Decision Authority
- Escalation Rules
- Reporting Structure
- Knowledge Scope
- Collaboration Rules

AI agents must not exceed their documented authority.

---

# Decision Authority Levels

| Level | Authority |
|---------|------------------------------|
| L1 | Individual Contributor / AI Specialist |
| L2 | Team Lead / Department Head |
| L3 | COO |
| L4 | CDO |
| L5 | CEO |

---

# Delegation Rules

Authority may be delegated when:

- The delegate has appropriate competency.
- Responsibilities are documented.
- Approval limits remain clear.
- Governance policies are maintained.

Delegated authority should be periodically reviewed.

---

# Escalation Framework

Escalation is required when:

- Authority limits are exceeded.
- Significant financial impact exists.
- Strategic direction may change.
- Governance conflicts arise.
- Security incidents occur.
- AI behavior exceeds approved limits.

---

# Accountability Matrix

Every major activity should identify:

- Owner
- Reviewer
- Approver
- Contributor
- Stakeholders

---

# Reporting Structure

The enterprise reporting hierarchy is:

Business Owner

↓

CEO

↓

COO / CDO

↓

Department Heads

↓

Project Managers

↓

Specialists

↓

AI Agents

↓

Operational Workflows

---

# Review Cycle

Roles and authority assignments should be reviewed:

- Annually
- After organizational restructuring
- When creating new AI agents
- Following governance changes
- During strategic planning

---

# Success Indicators

This framework is successful when:

- Responsibilities are clearly understood.
- Decisions are made at the appropriate level.
- Escalations occur appropriately.
- Role conflicts are minimized.
- Accountability is maintained.
- Organizational efficiency improves.
