# Purpose

The **00_GOVERNANCE** module defines the governance framework for VAYRA OS Enterprise.

It establishes the policies, decision-making authority, approval mechanisms, risk management practices, compliance requirements, and operational controls that guide the evolution of the enterprise.

Governance ensures that every business decision, architectural change, AI behavior, workflow, and operational process is managed in a controlled, transparent, and accountable manner.

---

# Mission

To provide a structured governance framework that enables VAYRA Atelier to operate with consistency, accountability, security, and long-term sustainability while supporting continuous innovation and responsible AI adoption.

---

# Objectives

The Governance module aims to:

- Define enterprise governance policies.
- Establish decision-making authority.
- Standardize approval processes.
- Manage organizational risks.
- Ensure compliance with internal standards.
- Govern AI behavior and automation.
- Promote accountability.
- Support transparent change management.
- Enable scalable business operations.

---

# Governance Principles

Every governance decision should follow these principles.

## Accountability

Every decision must have a clearly identified owner.

---

## Transparency

Governance processes should be documented and traceable.

---

## Consistency

Policies should be applied consistently across all departments and AI agents.

---

## Security

Business information and enterprise assets must be protected through appropriate governance controls.

---

## Continuous Improvement

Governance processes should evolve through regular reviews, feedback, and lessons learned.

---

## Responsible AI

AI agents must operate within approved authority, documented standards, and ethical guidelines.
