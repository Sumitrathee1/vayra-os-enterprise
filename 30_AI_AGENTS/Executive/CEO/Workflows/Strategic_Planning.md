---

# Purpose

This workflow defines the standardized process used by the CEO AI Agent to develop, evaluate, approve, and monitor the strategic direction of VAYRA Atelier.

Its objective is to ensure that every strategic decision aligns with the company's vision, mission, financial objectives, operational capabilities, market opportunities, and long-term growth plans.

---

# Scope

This workflow applies to all long-term strategic decisions including:

- Company Vision
- Business Expansion
- New Services
- Business Model Changes
- Technology Adoption
- AI Implementation
- Department Growth
- Market Expansion
- Revenue Growth Strategy
- Operational Improvements

---

# Objectives

The objectives of this workflow are to:

- Define long-term business direction.
- Identify growth opportunities.
- Improve organizational performance.
- Maintain competitive advantage.
- Support innovation.
- Increase profitability.
- Improve client satisfaction.
- Ensure sustainable growth.
- Build scalable business systems.

---

# Trigger Conditions

This workflow is initiated when:

- Annual strategic planning begins.
- Quarterly business review is completed.
- Market conditions change significantly.
- Business performance declines.
- Expansion opportunities are identified.
- Major investments are proposed.
- New technologies become available.
- Company vision requires revision.
